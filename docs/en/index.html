SmartCapture AI explained clearly.

It is not a generic motion detector. It is designed to decide better when a capture is actually worth taking.

It analyzes the scene in a light and consistent way

It works on an optimized luminance representation to keep decisions fast and stable without depending on huge resolutions.

It combines global, area-based, and local change

It does not rely on a simple image-to-image difference. It takes into account structural scene change and relevant motion in specific areas.

It accumulates signal and adjusts threshold

It can detect progressive change, compensate better for noise, and explain why it triggers instead of behaving like an opaque or arbitrary system.

Clear answers before you start.

Do I need a slider to use {{ identity.product_name }}?

No. You can start with phone-only timelapse and add slider motion later.

Does the app work with GRBL hardware?

Yes. It supports Bluetooth GRBL sliders with scenes, keyframes, and jog control depending on hardware.

Does remote control work over the Internet?

No. The remote panel works on a local network (Wi-Fi or hotspot) with a mandatory PIN.

What exactly can I do when exporting?

You can export MP4 or GIF in 720p, 1080p, or 4K depending on device; use Original/9:16/1:1 profiles; and apply text, timestamp, music, trim, speed, reverse, rotate, flip, loop, and fade in/out.
